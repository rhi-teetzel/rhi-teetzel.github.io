ABOUT

Hi, I'm Rhianna. I graduated in the spring of 2020 from a Software Development 14-week program at We Can Code IT, located in Cleveland, Ohio. I have always been passionate about technology and learning how to make things work. Here is a little backstory to give you a better idea of what I am all about!

I grew up playing on my dad's computer, which was initially a Windows 3.1, and it really was the little engine that could... I learned at a very young age to use DOS to open and execute my favorite games. That is when my passion for computers was born! I continued on that path for most of my childhood and teenage years, always tinkering with files, downloading mods and making things my own (sorry, dad!). It wasn't until my mid-twenties that the light bulb went off, and I made the decision to officially pursue something I've always truly loved.
Professionally, I have years of customer service experience- ranging from managing a laser tag facility, to front desk reception at a veterinary clinic, and all the way down to Orlando where I operated attractions at Walt Disney World!
After some time in college to originally pursue Graphic Design, and one Associate of Art degree later, I have finally pinpointed what I love most:
Using technology to CREATE!

Please do not hesitate to reach out if you have any questions, or even just to share stories-- I'd love to chat!
